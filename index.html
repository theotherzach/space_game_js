Controls & Glossary

Build

  • C Connector — extend the power network
  • D Miner — extracts from minerals in range
  • T Turret — shoots nearest enemy
  • L Laser turret — wider range, harder hits
  • B Booster — +25% rate to nearby miners/turrets
  • X Sell — refund 60% of build cost

Sim

  • Space Pause / unpause
  • 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 Pause / Slow / Normal / Fast
  • W Send next wave now
  • R Research panel
  • M Mute audio
  • Esc Cancel placement / close panel

Enemies

  • Raider Standard threat — moderate HP and damage
  • Scout Fast and fragile (wave 2+)
  • Tank Heavy armor, high damage (wave 3+)
  • Bomber Suicide AOE on contact (wave 4+)
All buildings must connect back to the base. Out-of-range = inert. Boosters stack.

Missions

Tech research is carried across missions.

Research
